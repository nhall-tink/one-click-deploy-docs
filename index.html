Demo Application Creation

We're going to have a demo app!

You can use this form to setup a demo app for your company, and Tink Integration

  1. Create a console.tink.com account and login
  2. Retrieve your client_id and your client_secret from the app settings page
  3. Use the form below, with the details from step 2/, and deploy!
  4. When the app is launched, complete the step to add the 'RedirectURI'
  5. Your demo is ready to use and be shared!

Reach out to solution.architecture@tink.com with any issues!
